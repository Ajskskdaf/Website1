AI Fellowship project

VisionPiano

A computer-vision and audio system that turns an ordinary webcam and a laptop mic into a real-time piano tutor. It watches an overhead camera feed to figure out which key and finger is being played, listens to the audio to confirm the note, and in Practice mode checks that against a reference song — all without needing a MIDI keyboard. Built as a hands-on substitute for a research paper's trained audio-visual model, using a click-based calibration step, a hand-landmark tracker, and a tuned pitch-detection pipeline instead.
